Before Your Visit

- Appointment Scheduling: Schedule your appointment in advance through the hospital's website, app, or by calling the appointment desk. - Medical Records: Gather all relevant medical records, previous test results, and a list of medications you are currently taking.
- Insurance Details: Check your insurance policy for coverage details and ensure you bring your insurance card and identification.

On the Day of Your Visit

- Arrival Time: Arrive at least 15-30 minutes before your scheduled appointment to allow time for registration and initial assessments.
- What to Bring: Bring your ID, insurance card, medical records, a list of medications, and any necessary authorization forms.

At the Hospital

Registration Desk: Upon arrival, go to the registration desk to check-in and complete any necessary paperwork.
- Patient Identification: Ensure you receive a patient ID bracelet which you should wear at all times during your stay.
- Waiting Area: Follow directions to the appropriate waiting area. The staff will call you when it’s your turn.
